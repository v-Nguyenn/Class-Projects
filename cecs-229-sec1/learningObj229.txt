Learning Objectives for CECS 229
Course Objectives:
1. Understand basic number theoretic concepts and perform simple number theoretic operations
2. Apply Chinese Remainder Theorem
3. Apply number theoretic operations in simple building blocks of public-key cryptography
4. Understand basic concepts in fields, vectors, and vector spaces, matrices, basis, and dimensions
5. Perform basic computations on vectors and matrices
6. Compute vector space bases, ranks, and null spaces of matrices.
7. Solve basic linear algebraic equations and understand their properties with emphasis on matrix transformation to an echelon form.
8. Understand basic concepts of inner product, orthogonalization, eigenvalue, eigenvectors, singular value decomposition and their properties.


Assignment 1:
- Understand and apply the concept of congruence in modular arithmetic
- Use algorithms to find all integers congruent to given value modulo m within a specified range.

Assignment 2:
- Convert numbers between decimal and other bases(up to 36)
- Do binary addition and multiplication in python

Assignment 3:
- Use the Sieve of Eratosthenes to generate prime numbers within a range
- Implement the Euclidean Algorithm to compute GCD of two integers.

Assignment 4:
